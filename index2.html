Internship at Gatsby

Gatsby is revolutionizing the Australian nightlife and beverage industry by providing cutting-edge real-time analytics and personalized venue-finding experiences. As a Full Stack Developer intern, I contribute to the development of features that enhance user interaction and venue management, leveraging the latest technologies in web application development.

This role has equipped me with hands-on experience in managing large-scale software projects, from initial design to deployment, and has honed my skills in coding, teamwork, and problem-solving.

What We Do

Gatsby leverages technology to transform how venues engage with patrons, offering tools that streamline promotions and enrich the customer experience. Our platform provides venues with powerful analytics to understand customer preferences, optimizing their marketing efforts and enhancing customer satisfaction.

Benefits for Venue Owners and Patrons

For venue owners, Gatsby's platform means deeper insights into patron behavior and preferences, leading to more tailored experiences that drive repeat business. Patrons enjoy a more personalized nightlife exploration, discovering venues aligned with their tastes and interests, which enhances their social outings.
